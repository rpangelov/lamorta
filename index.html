Biography

LaMorta started of in late 2014, with two musicians mutual love for heavy grooving Death-Metal in style of: Obituary, Hail of Bullets, Entombed AD. Their style seems familiar but still differs from others. Making use of different styles in heavy grooving Music. Their journey began to take shape when hitting the stage with bands like: Horrified (UK), Apocalipsis (MEX), Human Vivisection (BEL) and Rotten Casket (NL). Now in 2016 the next step is getting their, right in your face, metal out to the masses. Recording their first EP entitled "Crippled by War". Recorded in their own studio, in their own time and pace. It may not be perfect, but it's one hell of a ride.
